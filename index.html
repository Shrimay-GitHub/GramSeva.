‚ùì Frequently Asked Questions

Find quick answers to common questions

How do I report an issue in my village?

Simply click the "Submit New Request" button, fill out the form with details about the issue, add photos if needed, and submit. You'll receive a tracking ID to monitor progress.

How long does it take to resolve issues?

Response times vary by issue type. Emergency issues are addressed within 24 hours, while routine maintenance may take 3-7 days. You can track real-time progress using your request ID.

Can I track my submitted requests?

Yes! Use the "Track Request" button or visit the tracking page. Enter your request ID to see current status, updates, and estimated completion time.

What types of issues can I report?

You can report water supply issues, electricity problems, road maintenance, healthcare concerns, education facilities, sanitation issues, and other village infrastructure problems.

Is there a cost for using this service?

No, Village Connect is completely free for all residents. Our goal is to improve village infrastructure and quality of life for everyone in the community.

How do I report emergency issues?

For emergencies, use the red "Emergency Report" button in the Quick Report sidebar or mark your issue as "High Priority" when submitting. Emergency issues receive immediate attention.
